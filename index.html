Ahoy, internet traveller!

I am honoured by you visiting this website. My name is Sasha and these few pages contain lots of information about me.

I have a great many interests in my life, but my current prime occupation is being a PhD student at the University of Waterloo in Waterloo, Ontario, Canada. Throughout my PhD program I have been focusing on conversational and voice interactions between humans and computer systems. In particular, I have been studying how such interactions can be used to facilitate the process of information finding, and web search specifically. At one point, I endeavoured to explore how voice-based web search can be useful for people who are blind or visually impaired.
